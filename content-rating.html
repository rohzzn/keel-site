Content rating

Utility product, not mature media.

Keel is a productivity and personal finance utility for tracking subscriptions. It does not contain public social feeds or mature content.

Last updated: March 7, 2026

What the app does not contain

  • No graphic violence or violent imagery.
  • No sexual or suggestive content.
  • No gambling or betting content.
  • No illegal drug references or promotion.
  • No user-generated public social features.

What the app does contain

Users can enter private subscription details such as names, costs, billing dates, notes, and optional custom logos for their own account and device.

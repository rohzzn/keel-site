Financial features

Organizational finance utility, not a financial institution.

Keel helps users track subscription costs and renewal dates. It does not provide banking, payment processing, lending, insurance, or investment services.

Last updated: March 7, 2026

What Keel does

Keel organizes recurring subscription information entered by the user and presents that information through overview, calendar, and export tools.

What Keel does not do

  • It does not move or hold money.
  • It does not issue credit or process loans.
  • It does not provide investment recommendations.
  • It does not execute trades or manage assets.
  • It does not connect to banks to withdraw or deposit funds.

No professional advice

Any financial totals, reminders, or analytics shown in the app are for organizational awareness only and are not professional advice.

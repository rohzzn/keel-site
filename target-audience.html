Target audience

Built for self-managed subscription tracking.

Keel is intended for consumers who want to track recurring subscriptions, billing dates, and spending habits. The app is not designed for children.

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Intended audience

Keel is intended for adults and older teens who actively manage digital subscriptions, memberships, and recurring expenses.

Not directed to children

Keel is not designed for or marketed toward children under 13 and does not contain child-oriented characters, themes, or learning content.

Product context

The app focuses on personal organization and money awareness, which makes it more appropriate for users managing their own subscriptions and recurring charges.
